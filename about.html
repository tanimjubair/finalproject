Background

We often noticed that there were people who didn't know what smartphone to get and ended up buying a phone they later regretted buying. We wanted to make a difference on their choices. That's why we wanted to do something to help people, and this is how Smartphone.News was found. We first began this journey with 3 people and now, we have also gained people who we treat like family who has joined us on this journey to help fellow people. We are so glad!

Goal

Our goal is to inform our customers about the latest smartphones that is now popular among everyone in this day and age. We don't want our users to get a phone they first thought was good but later regretted buying.

Impact

People now can use our website to check what phones to get in this year so that they don't regret buying phones they didn't like!
